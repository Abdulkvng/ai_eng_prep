## What AI Enablement means

AI Enablement means helping engineering teams use AI effectively, safely, and responsibly.

It usually includes:

- building internal AI tools
- integrating AI providers
- improving AI workflows
- creating evals and guardrails
- monitoring usage, cost, latency, and quality
- helping teams understand when AI helps and when it does not

## Interview target

You want to sound like this:

> I can build an AI system end-to-end. I know how to connect an LLM to tools and data, evaluate if it works, monitor it in production, control cost, reduce risk, and explain the tradeoffs clearly.
